Chef's Recommendation

COFFEE ICE
Coffee that is served cold is known as kopi ais. It
may be made by either making coffee as usual and
serving it over ice or in cold milk, or it can be made
by brewing coffee cold. Sweeteners and flavors may
be added in hot brewing before chilling since they
dissolve more quickly.

TEH ICE
Teh ais refer to milky tea water mixed with ice. It
is a favorite drink in the tropics. Iced tea is often a
hot weather option, however, it remains
an all-time favorite for locals.

CHAM ICE
Cham is a cultural drink that is also known as Kopi Cham
in Malaysia. A combination of coffee and milk tea, where you
can taste the flavours of coffee and tea in one cup. Cham is an
alternative to your normal brew. And this drink satisfies both
coffee and tea lovers.

NESLO ICE
NESLO is a Malaysian drink loved by many, especially
those who love coffee. The name of this drink comes
from combining the names of the two other drinks that
make up Neslo to begin with— Nescafe and Milo.
